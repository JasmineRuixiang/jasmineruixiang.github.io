Welcom to my literature world!

#### __小说/Novels__
小说也许是最能同时贴近而又超越生活的艺术手法。不同于音乐的抽象，小说指向生命和生命的种种表征，而这也是最让我着迷的一点。

Novels might be the best artistic tools both reflecting/mirroring and transcending life as a unique art modality. Drastically different for its negation of musical abstraction, novels point directly at multifaceted features of life and living, by which I’m deeply mesmerized.



#### __诗歌/Poems__
说是诗歌也不见得，更多脱胎于自2013年左右开始的记录习惯，而在19年终于慢慢进化出了类似当代诗/散文的表达方式。有些欣喜，关于文字探索的热情也得到空前高涨，但在尝试的途中不免被文字牵扯着走。当代的诗歌有何意义？现实虚幻，物质精神，该怎样去认识并把握自己？时光如水，记忆逐渐被遗忘，在山穷水复的求索中，兴许文字能成为一种陪伴，渴望，与生活本身罢。

Emerging from my habit of note taking since 2013, it evolves and eventually crystallizes into a form representative of combination of modern poetry and prose. Ecstasy surges with my passion to explore with and within words, though with such unprecedented passion I realize I have been sometimes inevitably dragged along and away by words —— something to caution about. What makes it contemporary poem? Tantalizing among reality and fantasy, substance and spirit, how am I going to seize myself? Moreover, as time flows and flushes away with memory, poems might accompany as I crave and despair, perhaps as life itself.



#### __评论与想法/Comments and Thoughts__
零散的记录与随笔，对自己开诚布公。希望灵感与瞬间能被留住， 出于这样的想法。

Sporadic notes with loose structures, more importantly they need to be faithful for myself. Wish the moments for intuition and sparkles will be joyfully and passionately kept.
